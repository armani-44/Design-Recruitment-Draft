Privacy Policy

How we handle your data.

This is a placeholder privacy notice. The final policy will be drafted with our legal advisors and published before launch. The summary below outlines our intended position.

Who we are

Designer Recruitment® is a UK specialist recruitment agency exclusively serving the Kitchen, Bedroom, Bathroom & Builders Merchant industry. Contact: hello@designerrecruitment.co.uk.

What we collect

When you contact us, submit a CV, or post a role, we collect the information you provide (name, contact details, employment history, role requirements). We use it solely to match candidates with appropriate opportunities and to communicate with you.

How long we keep it

Candidate records are retained for the duration of an active engagement and for a reasonable period afterwards in case of follow-up opportunities. You can request deletion at any time.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data. Email us at privacy@designerrecruitment.co.uk.
