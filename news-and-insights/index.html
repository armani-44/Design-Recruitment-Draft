Chris Mitchell.

Independent Kitchen Design Consultant · 720studio.co.uk
"Technology, especially design software like Compusoft Winner, is a game-changer. It allows me to bring design concepts to life in 3D."

Chris's love for design started at school, where he built a skatepark using early 3D software. He went on to study architectural drawing and now consults independently on European and handmade kitchen furniture, recognised across the industry as a Compusoft Winner expert.

Read the feature

Faye Newman.

Designer · Faye Newman Design
"My favourite part about designing has to be the clients I get to meet. I find so much joy in being part of such an important aspect of their home project."

Faye specialises in in-frame kitchens, a style she's drawn to for its timeless quality and the intricate detail it allows. Every project is built around the client, with bespoke accessory and furniture choices that turn each kitchen into something genuinely personal.

Read the feature

Darren Morgan.

Designer · Darren Morgan Design
"He is absolutely fantastic at what he does, and well worth a call should you need additional design support."

Darren is an award-winning designer working across kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, and interiors. He partners with Designer Recruitment to give businesses on-demand access to high-calibre design expertise without the overhead of an in-house hire.

Read the feature

Langstaff -Ellis.

Bespoke Interiors · Oxfordshire
"A truly bespoke, luxury lifestyle business at every turn, with a seamless process from brief to execution."

Langstaff-Ellis has been crafting luxury bespoke interiors from Oxfordshire since 2008. Bringing together dedicated teams in design, craft, technical, and sales, they collaborate with leading architects and interior designers on exclusive residential projects across the UK.
