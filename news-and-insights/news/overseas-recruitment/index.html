Overseas Recruitment — Australia & Dubai.

Designer Recruitment has expanded its overseas recruitment service for KBB professionals, supporting placements across both Australia and Dubai within established, design-led businesses.

International placements 2 min read By Ben Paine

We work with trusted international partners to place experienced professionals into high-end residential, retail, and showroom environments, including roles such as Sales Designers, Kitchen Designers, Showroom Designers, and senior sales professionals.

A new path for UK talent.

Whether you're an aspiring sales designer, experienced designer, or senior sales professional, the opportunity to live and work in Australia or Dubai is now part of our offering. We support the full process, including introductions, relocation conversations, and ongoing coordination between candidate and client.

We are delighted to be able to offer this service to our network. Designer Recruitment will continue to explore new markets while maintaining the highest level of support for both clients and candidates.

If you're seriously considering a move overseas, or looking to hire internationally, please get in touch.
