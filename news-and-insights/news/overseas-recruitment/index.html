Overseas Recruitment, into Australia.

Posted 29 September 2023 2 min read By Ben Paine

Designer Recruitment has launched an overseas recruitment service for KBB professionals. We've partnered with a major Australian kitchen retailer to place designers into East Coast locations, including Sydney and the Gold Coast.

A new path for UK talent.

Whether you're an aspiring sales designer, an experienced kitchen designer or a senior sales professional, the opportunity to live and work on the Australian East Coast is now part of our offer. We handle the brief, the introduction, the relocation conversation, and everything in between.

"We are delighted we can offer this service to our network. Designer Recruitment will continue to venture into new areas and maintain the highest level of support for our clients and candidates."

Curious about a move overseas, or hiring from abroad? Get in touch directly, or explore the live roles in our jobs section.
