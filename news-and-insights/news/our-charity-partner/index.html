Charity Partner

Supporting BacZac's Legacy.

Designer Recruitment are proud to announce BacZac as our charity partner, supporting young adults and their families affected by cancer. BacZac is a charity that is particularly close to our founder's heart.

Our long-term charity partner 3 min read By Ben Paine

How BacZac supports.

BacZac delivers three key initiatives:

  • High street vouchers distributed to cancer units to support patients, along with meal vouchers for young adults undergoing chemotherapy treatment in Nottingham.
  • Small grants for families supporting young adults with cancer, helping with day-to-day costs or creating meaningful family experiences.
  • A long-term goal of providing respite holiday accommodation for families affected by cancer.

Our involvement.

We are proud to have supported BacZac over a number of years through their fundraising events, including the BacZac 6-a-side Charity Cricket Day in 2023 at Rushden and Higham Town Cricket Club, and the BacZac His Legacy Golf Day in 2025 at Overstone Park Hotel and Golf Resort.

Both events brought together supporters from across the UK to raise awareness and funds for the charity.

Ben Paine
"It was a time for everyone who knew Zac to come together to remember and promote the charity, and what it contributes to young adults and their families suffering from cancer."
Ben Paine
"Every year is a step in the right direction, and any sponsorship is welcome."
Ben Paine

To find out more about BacZac or to make a donation, please visit: baczachislegacy.org.
