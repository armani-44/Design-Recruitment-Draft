Langstaff -Ellis.

Bespoke Luxury Interiors · Oxfordshire
Posted 10 September 2023 Crafting interiors since 2008 Exclusive partner of Designer Recruitment
Langstaff-Ellis · Chelsea

Langstaff-Ellis has been crafting the finest living spaces from their workshop in Oxfordshire, since 2008. Dedicated teams across design, craft, technical and sales work together to bring every concept and vision to life.

Langstaff-Ellis is a truly bespoke, luxury lifestyle business at every turn; with a seamless process from brief to execution, a highly skilled and innovative team, and a vision for the future of interiors, it's no surprise that Langstaff-Ellis are working on projects with some of the most renowned Architects and Interior Designers in the industry, at some of the most exclusive addresses…

Designer Recruitment are proud to work in an exclusive partnership with Langstaff-Ellis and have helped them with their ongoing recruitment.

Watch this space, there will be much more to come from the fantastic team there.
