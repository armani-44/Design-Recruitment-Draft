Chris Mitchell.

Independent Kitchen Design Consultant · 720studio.co.uk
Posted 03 November 2023 Compusoft Winner expert European & handmade furniture
Chris Mitchell · 720studio.co.uk

Designer Recruitment recently caught up with Chris for a brief interview. Chris operates as an independent kitchen design consultant, offering specialised assistance to retail clients. His expertise lies in European and handmade furniture, and he's recognised across the industry as a Compusoft Winner expert.

01How did you get started in the world of design?

"My design journey started at school; I designed a skateboard park using first-generation 3D software. I've always been fascinated by the way a space can be transformed by design. After studying architectural drawing, I knew this was my true calling."

02How does technology, like design software, enhance your work?

"Technology, especially design software like Compusoft Winner, is a game-changer. It allows me to bring design concepts to life in 3D, making it easier for clients to visualise the end result. It also streamlines the design process, saving time and ensuring precision."

03What do you do outside of kitchen design?

"Outside of kitchen design, I'm a passionate mountain biking enthusiast. It's where I find inspiration, challenge myself, and experience the great outdoors in all its glory."
